#### 들어가기 전에 
- 문자열은 ***immutable***(변경 불가능한) 자료형!
```python

word = "apple"
print(word)
print(id(word))
>>> apple
>>> 1352749370800

word += "banana" 
print(word) 
print(id(word))  
>>> apple banana 
>>> 1352749417520
```
